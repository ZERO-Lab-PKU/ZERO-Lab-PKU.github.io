Biography

Dr. Zhouchen Lin is a Professor in Department of Machine Intelligence, School of Electronics Engineering and Computer Science, Peking University.

I am now recruiting Ph.D.s who have strong mathematical abilities (however, this does not imply that you have to come from mathematics department) and great interest in theoretical analysis in order to enjoy with me how to use mathematics to solve real problems elegantly. I am also recruiting Masters who have strong coding skills and interests in Quantitative Trading (a reference book). Welcome to send me your detailed resume!
