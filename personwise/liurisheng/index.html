Biography

I am currently a full professor with Digital Media Department at International School of Information Science and Engineering, Dalian University of Technology (DUT). I obtained my B.S. and Ph.D. degrees both from Dalian University of Technology, China, working with Prof.Zhouchen Lin. I was also doing joint PhD research in Carnegie Mellon University from September 2010 to January 2012.
