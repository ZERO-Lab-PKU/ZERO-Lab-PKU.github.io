Biography

I am an assistant professor at Capital Normal University. I completed my Ph.D. from Beijing Jiaotong University under the supervision of Prof. Zhouchen Lin and Prof. Jian Yu. I received the Excellent Ph.D. Thesis Award by Beijing Society of Image and Graphics in 2018.
