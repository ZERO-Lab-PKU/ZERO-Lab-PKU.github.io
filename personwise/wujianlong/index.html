Biography

I’m Jianlong Wu, a tenure-track assistant professor in School of Computer Science and Technology, Shandong University(Qingdao Campus). Before that, I received my Ph.D. degree in computer vision from School of Electronics Engineering and Computer Science, Peking University in 2019, advised by Professor Zhouchen Lin and Professor Hongbin Zha.
