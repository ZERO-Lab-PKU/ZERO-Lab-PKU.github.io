Correntropy Induced L2 Graph for Robust Subspace Clustering

We study the robust subspace clustering problem, and present a general framework from the viewpoint of half-quadratic optimization to unify the L1 norm, Frobenius norm, L21 norm and nuclear norm based subspace clustering methods.

Linearized Alternating Direction Method with Parallel Splitting and Adaptive Penalty for Separable Convex Programs in Machine Learning

We propose linearized alternating direction method with parallel splitting and adaptive penalty for efficiently solving linearly constrained multi-variable separable convex programs, which are abundant in machine learning.

A Counterexample for The Validaity of Using Nuclear Norm as A Comvex Surrogate of Rank

We conclude that even for rank minimization problems as simple as noiseless LatLRR, replacing rank with nuclear norm is not valid and LatLRR is actually problematic because the solution to its nuclear norm minimization formation is not unique in this paper

Rectification of Optical Characters as Transform Invariant Low-rank Textures

Geodesic based semi-supervised multi-manifold feature extraction

We have proposed a new multi-manifold learning algorithm, which combines semi-supervised multimanifold modeling, nonlinear feature extraction and a new semi-supervised discriminant analysis method to achieve better performance in geodesic feature extraction and classification tasks.

Fixed-Rank Representation for Unsupervised Visual Learning

Non-Negative Low Rank and Sparse Graph for Semi-Supervised Learning
